ABOUT

The Gardeners Cooperative was founded in Hanwell, London in 1939. The mission was to start a network of gardeners that could serve the community with low rates, so that everyone could afford to have a beautiful garden; while creating well paid & secure jobs for local people. Since then the company has grown to cover most of West London, purely through a non-hierarchical, non-exploitative employment model. We are a collective that is run by the community, for the community.
